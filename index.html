Thinking in Index.

Measuring how well countries are protecting their populations from environmental health risks, the Environmental Performance Index - EPI datasets are taken from Yale University.

The statistic includes 180 countries and their rankings on climate change performance and environmental health based on a combination of different indicators. The indexes presented in this data visualization page consists of:

Environmental Health - HLT
Air Quality - AIR, Water & Sanitation - H2O, Heavy Metals - HMT, Waste Management - WMG.
